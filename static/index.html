Novelly

crowdsourced story builder

“There is no greater agony than bearing an untold story inside you.”
Maya Angelou

Creating a community for collaborative storytelling.

Imagine a story curated by the internet, chapter by chapter.

Novelly is a new way to write a story by crowdsourcing chapters generated by users. With the minds of different disciplines and morals, we can create absurd and expansive worlds. The goal of this application is to have each chapter voted by the users and ultimately create a unique narrative.
