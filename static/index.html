“There is no greater agony than bearing an untold story inside you.”

Maya Angelou

Creating a community for collaborative storytelling.

Imagine a novel curated by the internet, chapter by chapter.

Let us reimagine storytelling. Let us share stories, ideas, characters, experiences.

Novelly is an application where writers are able to make a contribution in creating a shared novel. It doesn't matter if you're an established author, a programmer, a chef, or even a pre-schooler. Our mission is to create a writing format that allows everyone to commit to a story that is much more expansive and unique with the minds of people from many different disciplines.

Share your knowledge. Share your stories. Let us see how deep this rabbit hole can go.
